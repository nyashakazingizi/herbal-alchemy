Lecithin Capsule

Lecithin improves gallstone health by promoting bile balance and preventing the formation of cholesterol gallstones.

  1. Emulsifies Cholesterol in Bile

    2. Lecithin contains phosphatidylcholine, a phospholipid that emulsifies cholesterol in bile, keeping it dissolved and preventing it from crystallizing. When bile contains insufficient lecithin, cholesterol can precipitate, leading to gallstone formation.

  2. Improves Bile Composition

    3. Lecithin increases the ratio of phospholipids to cholesterol in bile, which improves bile solubility and reduces the risk of gallstone formation. A balanced bile composition is essential to prevent

  3. Reduces Gallstone Formation

    4. By preventing the crystallization of cholesterol in bile, lecithin reduces the formation of cholesterol gallstones, which are the most common type of gallstones.
