Super COQ-10 Capsule

  1. Antioxidative Properties
    2.

    After a stroke, the brain undergoes oxidative stress due to a lack of oxygen (ischemia) and subsequent reoxygenation (reperfusion). This can lead to cell damage. CoQ10, being a powerful antioxidant, helps neutralize harmful free radicals and reduce oxidative stress, which can minimize damage to brain cells and support healing after a stroke.

  2. Cellular Energy Production
    3.

    CoQ10 is essential for mitochondrial function and energy production. Brain cells require significant energy, especially after the trauma of a stroke. By boosting ATP production, CoQ10 can help brain cells recover and function more efficiently, potentially aiding in the repair of damaged tissues and improving cognitive function.

  3. Cognitive Function and Mood Support
    4.

    Stroke survivors often experience cognitive decline, including memory and focus issues. CoQ10 may support cognitive function by improving energy metabolism in the brain and reducing oxidative damage.

    Furthermore, because CoQ10 can positively affect cellular health, it may also improve mood and reduce depression, which is common after a stroke.
