Cordyceps Plus Capsule

Chronic bronchitis involves inflammation of the airways, and cordyceps is believed to have significant anti-inflammatory properties. Some studies suggest that cordyceps may help reduce inflammation in the lungs and airways, potentially easing symptoms like coughing and wheezing.

Cordyceps has been shown to modulate the immune system, enhance the body's ability to fight infections and possibly reduce the risk of secondary infections (like pneumonia) that can complicate chronic bronchitis. It may help improve overall lung function by supporting immune health.
