Premature aging refers to the appearance or onset of age-related changes in the body or skin before the typical age at which these changes are expected to occur. This can include the development of wrinkles, fine lines, loss of skin elasticity, graying or thinning hair, and other signs commonly associated with aging. Premature aging can affect both physical and mental health, often influenced by environmental factors, lifestyle choices, and genetics.
