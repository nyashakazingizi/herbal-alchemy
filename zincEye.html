Zinc Tablets

Zinc is essential for the proper functioning of photoreceptor cells (rods and cones) in the retina, which are responsible for capturing light and converting it into electrical signals. Adequate zinc levels support the proper functioning of these cells and help maintain clear vision.

Research has shown that zinc supplementation, in combination with other nutrients (like vitamin C, vitamin E, and lutein), can help reduce the risk of AMD progression, particularly in people at high risk.
