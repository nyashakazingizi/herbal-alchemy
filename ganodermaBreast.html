Ganoderma Plus Capsule

It may have adjuvant benefits in cancer care. The active compounds in ganoderma-such as polysaccharides, triterpenes, peptides, and glycoproteins-are thought to have various effects that could potentially assist in fighting cancer or improving the quality of life for cancer patients.

Ganoderic acid in Ganoderma, may slow the growth of tumors in certain cancers through inhibiting angiogenesis (tumors grow their own blood vessels to supply oxygen and nutrient) and reduces metastasis (the ability of cancer cells to migrate from their original location to other parts of the body).
