Royal Jelly Capsule

Royal jelly is a nutrient-rich substance produced by worker bees to feed the queen bee. The connection between royal jelly and sexual health is primarily based on its nutrient profile, hormonal influences, and traditional uses.

  1. Estrogenic Properties

    2. Royal jelly contains compounds that can act like phytoestrogens. Estrogen plays a key role in sexual desire and reproductive health in women. By supporting estrogen balance, royal jelly may help improve libido, especially in women experiencing hormonal imbalances (e.g., during menopause).

  2. Support Testosterone Levels

    3. Royal jelly is believed to support the production of hormones like testosterone, which also play a role in sexual desire. Testosterone is essential for both menand women, and in women, it helps maintain libido and arousal.

  3. Fertility and Reproductive Health

    4. Royal jelly has been used in traditional medicine to support reproductive health. II is believed to enhance the function of the ovaries, improve egg quality, and balance hormones involved in fertility, such as estrogen and progesterone. Although this is more related to fertility, improved reproductive health can indirectly support a healthier libido.
