Grape Seed Extract

Grape seed extract (GSE) combats aging through its powerful antioxidantive, anti-inflammatory, and cellular protective properties.

  1. The antioxidants in this product scavenge free radicals, which generally lead to oxidative stress causing cellular damage and premature aging.
  2. Oxidative stress accelerates the degradation of collagen, a structural protein vital for skin elasticity. By reducing oxidative stress, it helps maintain skin firmness and elasticity.
