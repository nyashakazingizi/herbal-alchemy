Ishine Capsules

I-Power Capsule contains a combination of antioxidants including blueberry anthocyanins, ginkgo extract, grape seed extract and lutein, which prevent the damage in retina caused by free radical attack. It improves eyesight, relieves eyestrain and prevents age-related macular degeneration.

Recommended Use:

  • For teenagers or adults who use eyes intensively and with eyestrains.
  • For those who need to prevent damage of retina by UV rays due to prolonged outdoor activities or work.
  • For the middle-aged and elderly with impaired eyesight.
  • For adults: 2 capsules each time, once daily;

    • For teenagers: 1 capsule each time, once daily.
