Propolis Plus Capsule

Propolis has been shown to support gastric mucosa! healing, which is essential for repairing damage caused by ulcers. Studies have suggested that propolis can stimulate the production of mucous in the stomach. This mucus layer acts as a protective barrier that shields the stomach lining from gastric acid and other harmful substances, which are major contributors to ulcer formation and progression.

Helicobacter pylori (H. pylori) infection is one of the main causes of gastric ulcers, as the bacteria disrupt the stomach lining and increase acid secretion. Propolis has shown antibacterial properties in laboratory studies, and some research suggests that it may help inhibit the growth of H. pylori in the stomach. Propolis serves as a complementary therapy alongside antibiotics, potentially helping to eradicate the bacteria more effectively and reduce the risk of ulcer recurrence.
