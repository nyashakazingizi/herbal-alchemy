Chronic Fatigue Syndrome is a complex, long-term condition characterized by severe fatigue that doesn't improve with rest and is not directly caused by any other medical condition. This fatigue is often accompanied by a range of other symptoms, and its severity can greatly impair daily functioning and quality of life. The hallmark symptom of CFS is profound fatigue that lasts for at least six months and is not explained by another medical condition.
