Soy Power Capsule

Soy Power contains soy isoflavone, Ganoderma and Angelicae Dahuriae Extract.

lsoflavones, which are plant-based compounds found in soy, are classified as phytoestrogens due to their ability to mimic estrogen in the body. Their regulatory effect on estrogen levels is attributed to their structural similarity to human estrogen, allowing them to bind to estrogen receptors (ERs).

  1. High Estrogen Levels

    2. When estrogen levels are high, isoflavones may act as anti-estrogens by competing with natural estrogen for receptor binding, thus reducing estrogen's effects.

  2. Influence on Estrogen Metabolism

    3. lsoflavones may affect the enzymes involved in estrogen metabolism, promoting the conversion of estrogen into less active forms and thus helping to balance overall levels.

  3. Impact on Hormonal Feedback Loops

    4. lsoflavones can influence the hypothalamic-pituitary-gonadal axis, which regulates estrogen production. By modulating this feedback loop, they help maintain hormonal equilibrium.
