A Cold Sore is a small, fluid-filled blister that typically appears on or around the lips, although it can also develop in other areas of the face or inside the mouth. Cold Sores are contagious and can spread through direct contact with the blister or the saliva of an infected person. They are often triggered by factors such as fatigue, stress, hormonal changes, or exposure to sunlight.
