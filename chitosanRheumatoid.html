Chitosan Capsule

Chitosan can bind to fats and some toxins in the digestive system. Though this is more related to fat absorption, it may help support kidney function by reducing overall waste buildup, which could help the kidneys process uric acid more efficiently.

High levels of uric acid are often linked to insulin resistance and metabolic syndrome, which can also affect cholesterol levels. Since chitosan has been shown to help reduce cholesterol levels, it may indirectly improve overall metabolic health / and reduce risk factors associated with gout.
