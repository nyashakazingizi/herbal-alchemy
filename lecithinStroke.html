Lecithin Capsule

  1. Support for brain cell repair and regeneration
    2.

    Lecithin, due to its high phosphatidylcholine content, is essential for the structure and function of cell membranes, including those of brain cells(neurons).After a stroke, brain cells can be damaged, and lecithin may support the repair and regeneration of these cells by improving membrane integrity and function.

  2. Improved brain function and cognitive support
    3.

    Phosphatidylcholine, derived from lecithin is a precursor to acetylcholine, a neurotransmitter that plays a crucial role in memory, learning, and overall brain function. Acetylcholine deficiency is often associated with cognitive decline, and supplementing with lecithin may help improve cognitive function and memory, which is especially important after a stroke.

  3. Fat Metabolism and Cholesterol Balance

    4. Lecithin plays a role in fat metabolism and helps break down fats in the liver. After a stroke, maintaining healthy cholesterol levels is important to reduce the risk of further strokes or cardiovascular problems. Lecithin can help in managing cholesterol and fat metabolism, which may reduce the risk of another stroke.
