Deep Sea Fish Oil Capsule

Deep Sea Fish Oil Capsule is rich in omega-3 fatty acids, which can reduce hepatic triglyceride synthesis, in turn, decrease the amount of fat stored in liver cells. Omega-3 fatty acids promote the breakdown of ratty acids (oxidation) in the liver, which reduces fat accumulation. They also improve the liver's ability to process and eliminate excess fat.
