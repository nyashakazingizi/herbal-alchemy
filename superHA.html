Super COQ-10 Capsule

The heart muscle (myocardium) requires a lot of energy to function efficiently. After a heart attack, the affected heart muscle can become weakened or damaged due to reduced blood flow. CoQ10 helps improve the energy production in heart cells by facilitating the production of ATP (adenosine triphosphate), which is the primary energy carrier in cells. This can support the healing process and help improve heart function after a heart attack.
