Super COQ-10 Capsule

CoQ10 plays a crucial role in the production of ATP in the mitochondria. By increasing ATP production, CoQ10 may help improve the energy supply to neurons, enhancing their function and possibly slowing the progression of dementia.

This is particularly relevant in Alzheimer's, where neurons in the brain are highly energy-dependent.

CoQ10 may help support neuroplasticity by providing the energy and protection needed for brain cells to maintain their functions and form new connections. This could contribute to improved cognitive function in individuals with early stages of dementia.
