Spirulina Plus Capsules

Spirulina is packed with a variety of antioxidants including B-carotene, zeaxanthin and phycocyanin.

  • Beta-carotene: A precursor to vitamin A, beta-carotene is a powerful antioxidant that plays a key role in protecting the eyes from oxidative damage and supports healthy vision.
  • Zeaxanthin: Spirulina contains zeaxanthin, an important antioxidant found in the retina that helps protect against harmful light and oxidative damage. It also plays a crucial role in protecting the macula, the part of the retina responsible for central vision.
  • Phycocyanin: This unique blue pigment in spirulina has potent antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps reduce oxidative stress and inflammation, both of which can contribute to the development of eye diseases.
