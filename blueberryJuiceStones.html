Blueberry Juice (High VC)

Drinking Blueberry Juice (High VC) helps us stay hydrated. Proper hydration is essential for diluting urine and flushing out minerals that could otherwise form stones.

Blueberries contain natural citric acid, which may help prevent the formation of certain types of stones, such as calcium oxalate and uric acid stones. Citrate binds to calcium in the urine, reducing the risk of crystal formation.

Blueberries are rich in antioxidants, especially anthocyanins, which may reduce oxidative stress and inflammation in the kidneys. These properties can help protect kidney tissues from damage caused by kidney stones or other kidney-related issues.
