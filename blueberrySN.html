Blueberry Super Nutrition

This product is rich in proteins, vitamins, minerals, dietary fibers and phyto-nutrients such as anthocyanins. They work jointly to improve the overall health of people with AIDS.

  • Protein: To maintain muscle mass and immune function.
  • Carbohydrates: For energy to sustain the overall health.
  • Healthy Fats: For energy and cellular function, including omega-3s.
  • Vitamins and Minerals: Key micronutrients like vitamin D, vitamin C, zinc, and iron support immune health and overall well-being.
  • Phyto-nutrients: Flavonoids exhibit mild antiviral properties. They could contribute to overall immune support and possibly help reduce the burden of viral infections in the body.
  • Small, Frequent Meals: To manage symptoms like nausea, and improve calorie intake.
  • Hydration: Essential to prevent dehydration from diarrhea or vomiting.
