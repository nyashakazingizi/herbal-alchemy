Cordyceps Plus Capsule

Cordyceps contains bioactive compounds like polysaccharides and cordycepin, which have been shown to have immunomodulatory effects. Rather than simply boosting immune function, Cordyceps may help balance the immune system--enhancing immune responses when they are weak and suppressing hyperactive immune responses that are characteristic of autoimmune diseases.

Cordyceps can promote the activity of regulatory T cells, which help maintain immune tolerance and prevent the immune system from attacking the body's own tissues. In autoimmune diseases, regulatory T cells often fail to function properly, contributing to the disease process.
