Blueberry Super Nutrition

This product is rich in vitamins, minerals, protein, essential fatty acids as well as phytonutrients such as polyphenols, flavonoids and -carotene.

  1. Anti-inflammatory and antioxidative Properties

    2. These nutrients have anti-inflammatory and antioxidative properties that help protect the body from oxidative stress and free radical damage, which can arise from environmental factors, normal metabolic processes, and increased demands during pregnancy.

    Oxidative stress can negatively impact both maternal and fetal health, so getting sufficient antioxidants is vital to reduce these risks.

  2. Supports digestive health

    3. This product is rich in fructo-Oligosaccharides (FOS), which are a type of prebiotic fiber that can support digestive health, improve gut flora, and provide other health benefits.

    Pregnancy often leads to digestive issues like constipation, bloating, and indigestion. FOS act as a prebiotic, which means they serve as food for beneficial gut bacteria. These beneficial bacteria, in turn, support healthy digestion.

    FOS can help improve bowel regularity and reduce the discomfort of constipation, which is a common issue during pregnancy due to hormonal changes and pressure on the intestines from the growing uterus.

  3. Promotes Healthy Gut Flora

    4. A healthy balance of gut bacteria is crucial for immune function, digestion, and overall health. FOS promote the growth of beneficial bacteria like Bifidobacteria and Lactobacilli, which can help maintain a balanced gut microbiome.

    A healthy gut microbiome has been associated with better immune function, which is important during pregnancy, as the immune system is somewhat suppressed to avoid rejecting the fetus.
