Calcium Capsule / Compound Marrow Powder / Compound Calcium Nutrient Powder for Adults / Children

People with lupus are at increased risk for osteoporosis and bone loss, partly due to the use of corticosteroids, which can deplete bone density. Besides, due to skin damage, they are not supposed to be exposed to direct sunshine. Ensuring adequate intake of vitamin D and calcium is essential for bone health. Both Calcium Capsule and Compound Marrow Powder contain calcium and vitamin D, which help enhance bone health in people with lupus. Compound Calcium Nutrient Powder for Adults/Children are featured with high absorption rate and low risk of inducing calcification in soft tissues.
