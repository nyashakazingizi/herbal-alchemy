Spirulina Plus Capsule

There is some evidence to suggest that spirulina can help modulate gastric acid secretion. Excessive stomach acid production is a significant factor in the development and exacerbation of gastric ulcers. By balancing gastric acid levels, spirulina may help reduce the irritation of the ulcerated stomach lining and relieve ulcer pain.

Spirulina is a rich source of essential nutrients, including vitamins, minerals, amino acids, and essential fatty acids. These nutrients play important roles in tissue repair, immune function, and overall digestive health.
