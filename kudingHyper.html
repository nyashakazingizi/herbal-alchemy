Kuding Plus Tea

Kuding and America Ginseng both have ability to promote vasodilation. When the arteries relax and widen, the vascular resistance is reduced and the blood pressure lowered.

The sympathetic nervous system is responsible for the "fight or flight" response and can influence heart rate and blood pressure.

Overactivity of the sympathetic nervous system can contribute to high blood pressure by increasing heart rate and constricting blood vessels. Both Kuding and American ginseng have been shown to have a modulating effect on the sympathetic nervous system.
