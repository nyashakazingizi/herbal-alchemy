Lung cancer is a type of cancer that begins in the lungs, which are part of the respiratory system and responsible for oxygen exchange. It is one of the most common and serious types of cancer worldwide. Lung cancer occurs when abnormal cells in the lungs grow uncontrollably, forming tumors that interfere with normal lung function. If not treated, these cancer cells can spread (metastasize) to other parts of the body.
